ABOUT US


We are Codex — a passionate group of first-year students from IIIT Kottayam, united by our love for technology, problem-solving, and innovation. Formed with the spirit of collaboration and curiosity, Codex is more than just a hackathon team — we’re a learning community. Whether it's coding, building creative solutions, or diving into new technologies, we’re always eager to explore, experiment, and grow together. Our mission is to build impactful projects, learn continuously, and challenge ourselves through hackathons, coding competitions, and real-world tech problems. Though we’re just starting out, we believe in thinking big and working hard. Welcome to our journey — this is just the beginning.
